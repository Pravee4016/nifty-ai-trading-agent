# Fyers Token Generation - Exact Steps

## You're at: https://myapi.fyers.in/dashboard

### Step-by-Step Navigation:

1. **Look for the left sidebar** or top menu
   
2. **Click on "API Dashboard"** or **"My API Apps"**
   - This might be under "API" or "Apps" section

3. **Find your app** (should be listed there)
   - Name might be something like "NIFTY-TRADING-AGENT" or your custom name

4. **Click on the app name** to open details

5. **Look for ONE of these buttons**:
   - "Generate Access Token"
   - "Get Access Token"  
   - "Token" tab
   - Under "Authentication" section

6. If you see a **"Generate Token"** button:
   - Click it
   - It might ask for your PIN or 2FA
   - Copy the generated token
